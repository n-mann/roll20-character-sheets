ABILITIES

Once per session, you may engulf someone in crows, devouring them. You don’t check for Fallout until you have at least 3 stress in a track. Those who can hear your Caw will follow one word commands from you. When someone takes a coin from you, they must obey a single word command you give them.

Once per scene you may act with supernatural speed and strength. Doing so again that scene costs 1 Claw stress. All doors unlock with your touch. Doors may also be locked so only you may pass. Once per session mask your face and voice to match someone who you have seen and heard. When you acquire a trinket from someone, you can always track their location.

When you harm someone, any witnesses will flee in terror or be paralyzed by fear, your choice. When you take Claw stress, burst into a cloud of ravens and reform somewhere within eyesight. When you Caw with mad jabbering, choose an effect on those who can hear: madness, pain, paralysis. Once per scene you may appear anywhere you can see. Describe how you got there.
